How it works

  1. 01

    Connect your wallet

    Use MetaMask or Coinbase Wallet on Base Sepolia. You need USDC and a small amount of ETH for gas.

  2. 02

    Approve USDC

    Your wallet signs a one-time approval for 1 USDC to the contract. This is a standard ERC-20 approval.

  3. 03

    Confirm purchase

    Call buy() on the contract. It atomically pulls 1 USDC to the treasury and delivers the next NFT to your wallet.

  4. 04

    NFT in your wallet

    Both transfers (USDC payment + NFT delivery) happen in the same transaction — true on-chain DVP.
